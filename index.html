Zero guesswork. All action. An awesome workplace culture in six months

Zero guesswork. All action. An awesome workplace culture in six months

The reason culture change yields unremarkable results is that leaders inadvertently focus too much on values and results and not enough on behaviours.

Please do not take this the wrong way. Values and results are important aspects of workplace culture, but it is behaviors that have the most important job: crystalizing and bringing values to life and creating actions that produce results. When behaviors are downplayed, precious time is squandered on guessing how to give life to values and achieve meaningful results. Worst of all, the results will never be consistent.

While the number of values and results organizations seek out can be in the hundreds, it takes just eight behaviors to build an awesome workplace culture. Whether the number of employees can be counted on one hand, or you are a global organization with workforce of more than a million employees, six months is all that is needed. Six months is enough for your employees to master the eight behaviors and practice these until habits form. Mastering these eight behaviours make an awesome workplace culture possible.

These eight behaviors mean zero guesswork, and all action.

Meet the CultureBlox

These eight behaviors are the building blocks of awesome workplace cultures: the CultureBlox.

Gratitude

It can start with a thank you or a compliment and forms a strong foundation for appreciating the smallest of positives even in the most negative of situations.

Compassion

It warms the soul even just thinking about the word “compassion”, an essential behavior taking to heart the general concern for and wellbeing of other people.

Simplicity

Simplicity is the antidote to complexity. Don’t be fooled by its name – Simplicity is no simple feat, but, surprisingly, the ultimate sophistication.

Transparency

Transparency provides a clear window with which to see through and establish an understanding without having to resort to one’s vivid imagination.

Focus

Just as the concentrated stream of light from a laser beam allows it to travel far, focus is what allows us to go far and reach our goals in any endeavor.

Competence

Often mistaken for having skills, competence takes hold only when skills have been acquired and applied correctly again and again at opportune times.

Courage

Contrary to conventional wisdom, courage is not the absence of fear, but the acknowledgement of it and the conviction to persist despite it.

Adaptability

Adaptability is associated with change, a scary ordeal for many, especially when it appears uninvited, but without it, progress simply would not exist.

Build towers. Rise above the competition

The CultureBlox stand strong on their own, but the real magic happens when these are stacked, and towers are built, just like kindergarten, but for adults. Building towers is how you bring your organization’s values to life and deliver the results you seek.

Rise above the competition as you build towers to create your awesome workplace culture. Here are some examples to show you how it works.

Trustworthiness

A popular and often non-negotiable value that comes to fruition when a tower is constructed from the compassion, transparency, and competence CultureBlox. Practicing these three behaviors consistently throughout your organization can help achieve desirable results such as retaining good employees, attracting top talent, fostering collaboration, establishing trusted partners, generating sales demand, maintaining brand recognition, and thwarting stiff competition.

Efficiency

Who wouldn’t want to streamline operations in their organization? This highly sought after value comes into play when the simplicity, transparency, focus, and competence CultureBlox are stacked together. When these four behaviors are practiced consistently throughout your organization efficiency improves curbing rising costs, speeding up slow logistics, maximizing profits, growing market share, and reducing your organization’s carbon footprint.

Innovation

There’s nothing like innovation to stay ahead of the pack, so let’s look at how to stack CultureBlox together to bring innovation into your organization. Innovation stems from focus, competence, courage, and adaptability. With consistent practice throughout your organization these behaviors contribute to desirable results such as keeping up with technology, staying innovative, overcoming emerging threats, capitalizing on latest trends, and thwarting stiff competition.

Forced or forgotten.
How towers fall

We live and breathe the CultureBlox. We’d be hypocritical if we didn’t. Compassion and transparency are important to us, so it would be remiss of us if we didn’t share this very important information with you.

Whilst building towers of CultureBlox is, let’s say, child’s play, things can and will go wrong.

Just as a child can stack too many blocks and a tower will come crashing down, values can not be brought to life and the desired results will not be achieved, if behaviors are forced, forgotten, or fake. Instead of building an awesome workplace culture, you may just find that you are on the path to the complete opposite – a toxic workplace culture.

Take focus, for instance. If forced, it becomes fixation. If forgotten, sadly, distraction takes its place. Neither efficiency, nor innovation can be brought to life if focus is replaced by fixation or distraction.

When behaviors are forced or forgotten, inevitably, towers will fall.

Some things happen by chance

Awesome workplace cultures, on the other hand, must be built.
