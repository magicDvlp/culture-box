Andrew Bycroft

Chief Executive Officer

Andrew’s career spans four decades and has had more twists than the plot of Rian Johnson’s “Knives Out” movies. From a physicist to an IT manager, cyber security expert, salesperson, author, business analyst, culture change expert, and CEO, Andrew has excelled in everything he tackles.

Despite Andrew’s continued success, he has always been extremely generous with his time going to great lengths to help other people make powerful positive changes in their life. Since the age of seven, Andrew has been demonstrating an aptitude for learning well beyond people twice his age. Even at that early an age, he was passionate about helping people discover practical building blocks they could use to make their world a better place. In the years since, these building blocks have evolved into the eight CultureBlox and enabled leaders and the their organizations reach heights they never even imagined they could soar to.

Andrew lives in Sydney, Australia with his two daughters and wife whom he met online by chance. Shortly thereafter he travelled to China to meet and marry her. Let’s not forget that almost always by his side is his loyal, but sometimes naughty, canine companion; a little poodle that you might catch a glimpse of in his videos.

Neelabh Sharma

Chief Innovation Officer

Driven by genuine curiosity for what makes a meaningful life, Neelabh has a passion for seeking out transparency in a world where authority figures often bury facts behind complex lies.

In a decade-long stint with the State Bank of India, Neelabh worked in a variety of roles ranging from negotiations, dispute resolutions, technical operations, cyber security to marketing, each time providing highly effective, yet simple solutions to problems that had been considered too complex to solve. While working there, he also rekindled his long-lost love for tennis, and pursued it competitively to become the local doubles champion. This sparked an interest in health and fitness, resulting in Neelabh becoming a certified strength and conditioning specialist which enabled him to help many people transform their health.

Since then, he has set his sights on better serving the world, focusing his efforts on tackling mammoth-sized problems that plague the world in the areas of education, food, water, air, and waste disposal, using the eight CultureBlox as a means to make a profound impact.

Neelabh lives in New Delhi, India with his wife and daughter, taking on time to home-school her whilst also offering personal care and ongoing support to his aged parents.

The belief.
A world with more solutions and fewer problems

Wells Fargo, Olympus, Theranos, Bank of America, Lehman Brothers, Volkswagen, and Enron are just a few of many.

It horrifies us every time another name makes front page news for all the wrong reasons; ending up on the “shame list” of organizations that chose to accept a workplace culture that resulted in wrongdoing and negatively impacting hundreds or thousands of innocent people.

We believe that if every leader worked to build an awesome workplace culture in their organization, we would have a better world with more solutions and fewer problems.

Our vision is to create a movement by inspiring leaders to do their part in building this better world.
