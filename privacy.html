We may enjoy a good laugh or joke. But your privacy is taken very seriously

This is the boring legal and technical mumbo jumbo, but it is important because, foremost it protects you and it also protects us.

In a sentence, what it all means is that we exemplify CultureBlox behaviors such as gratitude, compassion, transparency, focus, competence, and courage by going to great lengths to protect your identity and any information you provide to us, and if we fail we will come clean and let you know.

Data that is collected

Comments

If you leave comments on the CultureBlox website the data you submit via the comments form (name, email address and the message you send); the IP address of your computer, proxy server or VPN gateway; web browser software versions; and operating system software versions will be collected. Together this information helps separate humans from bots, it enables better detection of real messages and deletion of spam, and it also provides information about which content is most viewed. It allows us to serve you better with lots more content that is useful to you.

An anonymized string created from your email address (also called a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to see if you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: https://automattic.com/privacy/. After approval of your comment, your profile picture is visible to the public next to any comments you post.

Media

If you upload images to the website, you should avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS) included. Visitors to the website can download images and extract location data and will be able to pinpoint where the image was taken. There are freely available tools for removing location data.

Cookies

If you leave a comment on the CultureBlox site you may opt-in to saving your name, email address and website in cookies (no, not the kind you dunk in milk). These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again when you leave another comment. These cookies will last for one year.

If you visit our login page, a temporary cookie will be created to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

Unfortunately, we don’t have control over those sites, so it is important that you know that these websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Analytics

Google Analytics is used for website profiling. This provides information such as the number of pages viewed, the estimated view time of each page, IP address information of either your computer, your proxy server or VPN gateway and web browser and operating system software versions. This information is for us to identify which pages are most viewed and to optimize future content for you. This information is generic and is not attributed to any single user who visits the CultureBlox website.

Who your data is shared with

We do not voluntarily share your data with anyone outside of CultureBlox unless you request us to and give us consent in writing. If required, by law, to provide information we have collected about you to a court or law enforcement agency, you will be notified.

How long we retain your data

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata (such as the date and time the comment was posted) are retained indefinitely. This is to recognize and approve any follow-up comments automatically instead of holding them in a moderation queue. For users that register on our website, personal information they provide in their user profile will be stored. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (although they cannot change their username). Website administrators can also see and edit that information.

What rights you have over your data

At any time, you can request that any personal data held about you be deleted. This does not include any data we that must be kept for administrative, legal, or security purposes. If some or all of your data is unable to be deleted for these reasons, you will be informed.

Where your data may be sent

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service. We have no control over how that service handles the contents of your comments.

Data breach disclosure requirements

Whilst everything in our power is done to protect your data, if a data breach was to occur and information you have supplied to us is lost or stolen, the leaders at CultureBlox will:

1. Notify you and apologize within 24 hours of the breach being discovered. You will receive details of the breach and recommended next steps.
2. Publish information on its website to keep both you and the public informed of updates and actions being taken.
3. Notify law enforcement in the countries where CultureBlox operates.
4. Notify any privacy regulatory bodies in the countries where CultureBlox operates.
